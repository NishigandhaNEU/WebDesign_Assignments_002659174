ABOUT CASA BLANKA

Superb fusion of Algerian/Moroccan and West Mediterranean (Spain, France) Culinary "Savoir Faire"

Casablanca the Restaurant (former Marrakesh Location) in Costa-Mesa is Orange County's landmark of Algerian/Moroccan and West Mediterranean fusion cuisine, offering inspiring cuisine, live belly and flamenco dancing and pianist shows, to bring a unique culinary adventure in time and space to the West Mediterranean.

"I have come full circle and now I am that child coming from the kitchen with all the memories of the wonderful cuisine of my childhood. I am humbly emulating the cooking of my grandmother, mother and sisters, and the rest of the women from my culinary heritage." The Amokranes
